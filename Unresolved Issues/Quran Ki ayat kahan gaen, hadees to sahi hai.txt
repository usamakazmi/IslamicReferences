Sahih Muslim Hadith No. 3598
Chapter 18	The Book Of Suckling
Book	Sahih Muslim
Hadith No	3598
Baab	Razaat Ke Ehkaam O Masail

Amra reported that she heard 'A'isha 
(Allah he pleased with her) discussing 
fosterage which (makes marriage) 
unlawful; and she ('A'isha) said: 
It was revealed in the Holy 
Qur'an ten clear sucklings, and 
then five clear (sucklings).
