Sahih Bukhari Hadith No. 269
Chapter 5 The Book Of Ghusl 
(Washing Of The Whole Body)
Book	Sahih Bukhari
Hadith No	269
Baab	Ghusl Ke Ehkaam O Masail

Narrated `Ali: I used to get emotional 
urethral discharge frequently. Being 
the son-in-law of the Prophet I 
requested a man to ask him about it. 
So the man asked the Prophet about it. 
The Prophet replied, Perform ablution 
after washing your organ (penis).
