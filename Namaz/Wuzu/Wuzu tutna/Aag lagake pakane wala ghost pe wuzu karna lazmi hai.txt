It was narrated that 'Aishah said:
"The Messenger of Allah said: 'Perform ablution after (eating) that which has been changed by fire.'"

Grade:	 Sahih (Darussalam)		
Sunnah.com reference	 : Ibn e Majah Book 1, Hadith 220
English translation	 : Vol. 1, Book 1, Hadith 486
Arabic reference	 : Book 1, Hadith 524

Abu Hurairah reported:
The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: Perform ablution after eating anything which has been cooked by fire.
