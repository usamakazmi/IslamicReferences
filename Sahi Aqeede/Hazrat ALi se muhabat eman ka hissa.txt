Muslim 240 Chapter 1

Zar ibn Habish (Asadi Kofi who died at the age of one hundred and twenty or thirty or twenty-seven years and he saw the age of ignorance): Syedna Ali said: I swear by the one who cut the grain ( Then he planted grass and made life. The Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) promised me: "No one will love me except the believer and no one will hate me except the hypocrite."
