Sahih Bukhari Hadith No. 1072
Chapter 18	The Book Of Prostration 
During The Recitation Of The Quran
Book	Sahih Bukhari
Hadith No	1072
Baab	Sajood E Quran Ke Masail

Narrated `Ata' bin Yasar: I asked Zaid 
bin Thabit about prostration on which 
he said that he had recited An-Najm 
before the Prophet, yet he (the 
Prophet) had not performed a 
prostration.
