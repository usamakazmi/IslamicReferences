Imam Muslim Sahih Muslim k muqadme main likhte hen k sahi Ahadees itni hen k zaeef ahadees ka sahara lene ki
zaroorat hi nae. Aur jo log apna ilm ka rob jharne k liye logon k zaeef ahadees sunate phirte hen wo jahil 
hen aur inko ilm ki dawat dene se behtar hai k inko jahil bola jae. Yeh ishara ap ne imam Ahmed bin hambal 
ki taraf kia hai, isi waja se inhone(Imam Muslim) aur Imam Bukhari ne koi Hadees Imam Ahmed aur Imam Abu 
Hanifa se nae lin, imam Sha'afi se bhi ahadees nae li gaen kunke unke sath kuch logn ne sazish kareke unke 
character ko badnam kia gya tha isliye, isi tarhan aur bhi Namwar Muhaddis hen jo tiba tabaeen main se hen 
jinse ahadees nae li jaatin.
