Sahih Bukhari Hadith No. 3328
Chapter 61	The Book Of The Stories Of The Prophets
Book	Sahih Bukhari
Hadith No	3328
Baab	Anbiya Alaih Salam Ke Bayan Main

Narrated Abu Salama: Um Salama said, Um Sulaim 
said, 'O Allah's Apostle! Allah does not refrain 
from saying the truth! Is it obligatory for a 
woman to take a bath after she gets nocturnal 
discharge?' He said, 'Yes, if she notices the 
water (i.e. discharge).' Um Salama smiled and 
said, 'Does a woman get discharge?' Allah's 
Apostle said. 'Then why does a child resemble 
(its mother)?
