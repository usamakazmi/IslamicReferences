Abd al-Rahman ibn Sakhr Ad-Dausi Al-Zahrani, ya Hazrat Abu Huraira Hazrat Muhammad(s.a.w)
k sahaba main se hen in se kamse kam 5374 ahadith milti hen inhone ne 2-3.5 saal Ap(s.a.w)
k sath guzare.

Muhammad(s.a.w) ne apki mohabbat momino pe lazmi ki):

Ap k fazilat ki misal wohi hai k ap ne jab apni maan k zulm se tang ake Allah k Rasool se apni
maa k liya dua karwai, dua kahtam hote hi ap apne ghar ki taraf bhaage, iman k taqaza to yeh tha k forun hi qabool 
hojaegi ALlah k rasool ki dua, jab ghar puhache to amma ne awaz di k bahir ruko main gusal kar rahin hoon. Jab wo 
bahir aaen to bolin mujhe ALlah k rasool k pass lechalo mujhe Islam qabool karna hai. Jab dono Allah k rasool k pass
puhanche to Allah k rasool ne dua farmai k a Allah Abdul Rehman(Abu Huraira) aur inki maa ki mohabbat har monin ki 
dil main dal.
To log ye janlen k inki mohabbat bhi humpe ussi tarhan farz hai jese Hazrat Ali ki muhabbat hai, Allah se dareen aur 
ilm hasilkaren.
